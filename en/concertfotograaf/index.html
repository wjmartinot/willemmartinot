Concert photography & festival photography

Professional concert photography for international festivals in The Hague, Amsterdam and Rotterdam.

For more than 15 years I have been working as a concert photographer in collaboration with MOJO Concerts, photographing international artists and large-scale productions across major venues and festivals. One of the recurring highlights in my career is the North Sea Jazz Festival, where I regularly capture performances, backstage moments and the unique atmosphere of live music.

Concert photography requires speed, anticipation and a strong understanding of stage lighting and movement. Every show is different — dynamic lighting, fast-changing scenes and high energy performances demand experience and precision. My goal is always to capture the emotion, intensity and character of the live moment.

My images are used for promotion, press, marketing, archives and publications. Having extensive experience in large productions allows me to work smoothly within production teams and adapt quickly to changing conditions on stage and behind the scenes.

In addition to concerts, I also photograph festivals and live events throughout the Netherlands and internationally, delivering consistent quality and reliable production support.

If you are looking for an experienced concert or festival photographer for your production, feel free to get in touch to discuss your project.
