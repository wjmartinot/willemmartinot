Frequently asked questions

What does an event photographer cost in Rotterdam? + −

Event photography starts from €650 for a half day. A full day or conference is typically €850 to €1,250, depending on duration, location, number of photographers and delivery requirements. Multi-day congresses and rush delivery are quoted individually. See rates for an indication.

How do you choose the right event photographer? + −

Look not only at the portfolio but especially at experience with similar corporate events. A strong event photographer anticipates key moments (speakers, interaction, networking), works unobtrusively and delivers images suitable for LinkedIn, press and internal communications. Check experience with conferences, delivery speed and clear communication in advance.

How quickly do you deliver event photos? + −

An initial selection can often be delivered the same day or within 24 hours — ideal for social media, newsletters and press releases. The full retouched set usually follows within two to five business days, depending on event size. Rush delivery is available by arrangement.

Do you photograph at Ahoy, Kop van Zuid and congress centres in the Rijnmond? + −

Yes, as an event photographer in Rotterdam I have extensive experience at venues including Ahoy, Kop van Zuid and congress centres in the Rijnmond. I know the logistics of large venues and work discreetly and effectively without disrupting the programme.

How many photographers do you need for a conference? + −

For smaller events and single-track one-day conferences one photographer is usually enough. For larger congresses with multiple rooms, parallel sessions or a large audience I recommend a second photographer so no key moments are missed.

Do you work outside Rotterdam? + −

Yes, I regularly work in The Hague, Amsterdam and internationally. My base is in The Hague; I am flexible across the Netherlands.

Do you work discreetly during the programme? + −

Yes. I move unobtrusively through the space, use silent shutters where possible and never block speaker and audience. The event should run undisturbed; you mainly notice my presence afterwards in the quality of the images.

How many photos will I receive after an event? + −

It depends on duration and programme. For a half day you typically receive 80 to 150 retouched images; for a full day or conference 150 to 300 or more. We agree in advance on volume and which moments have priority.

Do you deliver photos suitable for press and social media? + −

Yes. Besides high-resolution images for print and archive I deliver optimised versions for LinkedIn, Instagram, newsletters and press releases. A quick first selection can be delivered the same day for live posting during the event.

What is the difference between an event photographer and a conference photographer? + −

In practice the same role: a photographer who documents business meetings, conferences and corporate events. I cover both atmosphere, speakers, networking and detail shots — suitable for communication, PR and internal use.
