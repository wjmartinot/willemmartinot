Veelgestelde vragen

What does a portrait shoot cost in Rotterdam? + −

An individual portrait session starts from €250. The exact price depends on duration, location, number of people and image usage. For teams and organisations I provide a custom quote. See rates for an indication or request a non-binding quote.

How do I book a portrait shoot? + −

Contact me via the contact form or email. We briefly discuss your requirements, preferred location and scheduling. You then receive confirmation with date, time and practical information.

How quickly can a shoot be scheduled? + −

In many cases a portrait shoot can be scheduled within a few days to two weeks, depending on availability. For urgent assignments I look at short-notice options — feel free to get in touch.

I don't like being in front of the camera — is that a problem? + −

No, especially then. Most people are not models and feel uncomfortable on camera. During the shoot I guide you calmly with clear direction on posture and expression. The goal is a natural portrait that looks professional, without a stiff or forced appearance.

How long does a portrait shoot take? + −

An individual session usually takes 30 to 60 minutes. For teams or multiple employees I allow 15 to 20 minutes per person, depending on the number of images and locations. We plan enough time so no one feels rushed.

What should I wear? + −

Choose clothing that fits your professional image: calm colours, simple patterns and a well-fitting outfit. Avoid large logos or distracting prints. Bring a second outfit if you like. I am happy to advise beforehand on what works best for your sector and purpose.

Where does the portrait shoot take place in Rotterdam? + −

On location (e.g. your office in Rotterdam, outdoors in the city) or in a studio environment in the region. For teams I regularly come on site with a mobile studio setup. Together we choose what best suits the desired look.

Which is better: studio or location? + −

A studio gives a calm, consistent look with control over light and background — ideal for LinkedIn and team portraits. A location adds context and personality and can strengthen your organisation's story. Both deliver professional results; the choice depends on the purpose.

Where can I use the portrait photos? + −

Portraits are used for LinkedIn, websites, presentations, press material, annual reports and internal communications. Images are delivered in high resolution for print and optimised versions for web and social media.

When will I receive the photos? + −

After the shoot I make a careful selection and professionally retouch the final images. You usually receive the photos within one business day via a secure download link (Google Drive). For larger series delivery may take slightly longer — we agree this in advance.

Can I use the photos freely? + −

Yes, for the agreed usage (website, LinkedIn, publications, internal communications). Usage rights are set out in the quote. Extended usage — e.g. for advertising or large print runs — can be arranged by agreement.

Can you photograph multiple employees? + −

Yes, I regularly provide portrait shoots for teams and organisations in Rotterdam and the surrounding area. This can be at the office with a mobile studio or at a central location. I plan the day efficiently so each employee has enough time without disrupting work.

Do you ensure a consistent look across a team? + −

Yes. Through consistent lighting, background and retouching all portraits get a uniform, professional look. That matters for websites, LinkedIn pages and annual reports where employees appear side by side.

What is the difference between a business portrait and a LinkedIn headshot? + −

A business portrait is more broadly usable (website, press, presentations). A LinkedIn headshot is specifically optimised for the LinkedIn format: sharp appearance, neutral background and composition that works well in a small profile circle. I offer both; often one session produces images suitable for both purposes.

Can the shoot take place at my office in Rotterdam? + −

Yes, that is common. I come on location with compact equipment and set up a mobile studio in a meeting room or office if needed. Employees don't need to travel and the shoot fits into the working day. See also my work as photographer in Rotterdam.

What file formats do you deliver? + −

You receive images digitally in JPEG (high quality, suitable for web and print) via a download link. On request I also deliver TIFF or other formats. All images are professionally retouched and ready for immediate use.
