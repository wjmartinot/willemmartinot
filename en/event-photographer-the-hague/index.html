Frequently asked questions

What does an event photographer in The Hague cost? + −

An event photographer in The Hague typically costs between €500 and €1250, depending on the duration of the event, the number of photographers and the desired delivery. For conferences and corporate events, pricing is usually tailored to the programme and key moments.

How do you choose the right event photographer? + −

You choose the right event photographer not only on portfolio, but especially on experience with similar business events. A strong corporate event photographer understands how to anticipate key moments (speakers, interaction, networking), how to work unobtrusively without disrupting the event, and which images suit LinkedIn, press and internal communications. Look for experience with conferences, seminars and corporate gatherings, a consistent style, speed of delivery and good communication upfront.

How quickly do you deliver event photos? + −

A first selection of images can often be delivered the same day or within 24 hours for use in communications and social media.

Do you also photograph at the World Forum or Fokker Terminal? + −

Yes, as an event photographer in The Hague I have experience photographing conferences and events at venues such as the World Forum and the Fokker Terminal.

How many photographers do you need for a conference? + −

For smaller events one photographer is often enough. For larger conferences or multiple rooms, a second photographer is recommended to capture all important moments.

Do you also work outside The Hague? + −

Yes, I regularly work in cities such as Rotterdam, Amsterdam and internationally, depending on the assignment. The Hague is my home base, but I am flexible and available throughout the Netherlands.
