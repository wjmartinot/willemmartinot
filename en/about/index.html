About Willem Martinot

I am Willem Martinot, a photographer based in The Hague. For more than twenty years I have helped entrepreneurs, professionals and organisations turn identity, values and story into strong, authentic imagery.

My background in film shapes how I work: light, composition and visual storytelling are central to every assignment. The result is photography that communicates credibility and consistency — whether for business portraits, branding campaigns, events or fashion editorials.

Clients have included Yamaha, Douwe Egberts, Hotelschool The Hague, C&A, Louis Philippe and Mojo, alongside independent professionals and creative agencies. Large productions and focused individual shoots receive the same level of attention and care.

I primarily work in The Hague, Amsterdam and Rotterdam, and regularly take on projects throughout the Netherlands. By combining creativity with efficient planning and realistic budgets, collaborations stay enjoyable and consistently high in quality.

Curious what I can create for your organisation or personal brand? Feel free to get in touch for an introduction.
