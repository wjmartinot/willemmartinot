Frequently asked questions

How much does a portrait shoot in The Hague cost? + −

The price of a portrait shoot depends on duration, location and how the images will be used. For business portraits I provide a clear quote upfront, tailored to your needs.

How can I book a portrait shoot? + −

You can easily get in touch via the website or by email. I briefly discuss your requirements, location and schedule, then we set a date.

How soon can a shoot take place? + −

In many cases a portrait shoot can be scheduled within a few days, depending on availability and location.

I don't like being in front of the camera — is that a problem? + −

No. Most people are not models. During the shoot I guide you calmly and clearly, so your posture and expression stay natural.

How long does a portrait shoot take? + −

An individual shoot usually takes between 30 and 90 minutes, depending on the purpose and number of images.

What should I wear for the shoot? + −

Choose clothing that suits your professional image. Neutral colours and simple combinations often work best. We align on this beforehand.

Where does the portrait shoot take place? + −

This can be on location (for example at the office or outdoors in The Hague) or in a studio setting. The choice depends on the look you want.

Which is better: studio or on location? + −

A studio gives a calm, consistent look. A location adds context and personality. Both options have their own advantages.

What can I use the portrait photos for? + −

Portraits are often used for LinkedIn, websites, presentations, press material and internal communications.

When will I receive the photos? + −

After the shoot I make a selection. The final images are professionally retouched and usually delivered within one business day via Google Drive.

Can I use the photos freely? + −

Yes, for the agreed use (for example website, LinkedIn or publications). Any additional use can be agreed in advance.

Can you photograph multiple employees as well? + −

Yes, I regularly carry out portrait shoots for teams and organisations, both on location and with a mobile studio.

Do you ensure a consistent look within a team? + −

Yes, by using consistent lighting, background and retouching, all portraits get a uniform and professional look.
