Frequently asked questions

What does a LinkedIn headshot cost? + −

A LinkedIn headshot session starts from €250. For teams and multiple employees I provide a custom quote. Price depends on location (studio or on site), number of people and retouching. See rates for an indication.

How long does a LinkedIn photo session take? + −

Usually 30 to 45 minutes per person. For teams I plan around 15 minutes per employee with an efficient flow. There is enough time to try several poses and backgrounds.

Where does the session take place? + −

In my studio in The Hague or on location at your office across the Randstad. For teams I regularly set up a mobile studio in a meeting room.

How quickly will I receive my photos? + −

Usually within one business day after the session. You receive a selection of professionally retouched images via download link, ready for LinkedIn upload.

I don't like being in front of the camera — is that a problem? + −

No, most professionals feel uncomfortable on camera. I guide you calmly with clear direction. The goal is a natural, professional portrait — not a stiff passport photo.

What should I wear for a LinkedIn headshot? + −

Professional clothing that fits your sector: calm colours, well-fitting outfit, no large logos or busy patterns. Bring a second outfit if you like. I am happy to advise in advance.

Do you photograph LinkedIn headshots for teams? + −

Yes. I regularly run sessions for teams and organisations with a consistent look through fixed lighting, background and retouching. Ideal for company pages and uniform employee profiles.

How is this different from a regular portrait? + −

A LinkedIn headshot is optimised for the LinkedIn format: sharp appearance, neutral background and composition that works in a small profile circle on desktop and mobile. More on corporate portrait photography.

Can I use the photo on other channels? + −

Yes, for the agreed usage (LinkedIn, website, email signature, presentations). Usage rights are set out in the quote.

Do you work outside The Hague? + −

Yes, I work on location in Amsterdam, Rotterdam and across the Randstad. The The Hague studio is also easy to reach from the whole region.
