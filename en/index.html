Business portraits and LinkedIn headshots

A strong professional image starts with clear visuals. As a portrait photographer in The Hague, I create business portraits for professionals, executives and teams — suitable for websites, publications and branding. For a quick profile update you can also book a LinkedIn headshot.

Strong business photography shows who an organisation is and how it works. That includes representative portraits as well as authentic workplace images that support a consistent visual language. We align beforehand on what story the photos should support.

Corporate event photography for companies and organizations

Timing and overview play an important role during events. I photograph business meetings, conferences and corporate events with attention to atmosphere, interaction and key moments.

In addition, I have many years of experience in the concert and festival industry, working with organisations such as Mojo and events like North Sea Jazz. This experience helps me work efficiently in dynamic environments and deliver top quality even in challenging light.

Active in The Hague, Amsterdam and Rotterdam

My base is in The Hague. I also regularly work in Amsterdam, Rotterdam and the rest of the Randstad. This central location makes it easy to combine assignments at different locations across the Netherlands.

Workflow and fast delivery of visual content

We make clear agreements beforehand about costs, purpose, schedule and how the images will be used.

  • Fast delivery: For social media and news updates, I can often deliver a first selection of images the next working day.
  • Post-processing: All images are professionally edited and delivered in high resolution for print, plus optimised versions for web use.

Frequently asked questions

What type of photography do you offer? + −

I specialise in corporate photography: event and conference photography, business portraits, corporate reportage and LinkedIn headshots. I also shoot fashion editorials and concerts. Each discipline has its own approach, but the foundation is the same: professional images ready for communication, branding and publication.

Who are your clients? + −

I work with multinationals, embassies, government organisations, cultural institutions and independent professionals. Clients include North Sea Jazz, FS-ISAC, DP World, Yamaha, Douwe Egberts and Schippers Architects — for both one-off assignments and ongoing collaborations.

Where are you based? + −

My studio and home base are in The Hague. From this central Randstad location I work on location weekly in Amsterdam, Rotterdam and other cities across the Netherlands. For international assignments I travel by arrangement.

Do you work outside The Hague? + −

Yes, regularly. I photograph weekly in Amsterdam and Rotterdam and am available throughout the Netherlands. The Hague is my home base, but most assignments take place on location at the client's venue — from congress centres and offices to events and studios.

How does a photoshoot or assignment work? + −

We start with a short briefing: what is the purpose of the images, who is the audience and where will they be used? Then we align on planning, location and any specific requirements. During the shoot I work efficiently and unobtrusively. Afterwards I make a selection, professionally retouch the images and deliver them digitally — tailored for web, print or social media.

How do you ensure consistent quality? + −

Through 25+ years of experience, thorough preparation and a consistent approach to lighting, composition and retouching. For recurring assignments I align style and look with the organisation's brand guidelines, so all images remain recognisable and professional — regardless of location or lighting conditions.

How quickly do you deliver images? + −

It depends on the assignment. For events, an initial selection can often be delivered the same day or within 24 hours — useful for social media and press. Portrait photos are usually delivered within one business day. The full retouched set follows within the agreed timeframe, typically within a few days.

What does corporate photography cost? + −

Rates vary by assignment type. Business portraits start from €250, event photography from €650 and corporate reportage from €950. See the rates page for an indication; for larger or bespoke assignments I always provide a clear custom quote.

Do you photograph conferences and corporate events? + −

Yes, conference and event photography is one of my core specialities. I photograph business meetings, conferences and corporate events in The Hague (including World Forum, Fokker Terminal), Amsterdam (RAI, Zuidas) and Rotterdam (Ahoy, Kop van Zuid). See my event photography page for more information.

Do you offer LinkedIn headshots? + −

Yes. I create professional LinkedIn headshots in my The Hague studio or on location at your office. Sessions are efficient (usually 30–45 minutes) and images are often delivered within one business day. Also suitable for teams and organisations across the Randstad. More info on the LinkedIn portrait photographer page.

How do I request a quote? + −

Via the contact form or by email. Briefly describe the type of assignment, preferred date and location. I typically respond within one business day with a proposal or a few targeted questions. Quotes are non-binding.

How many years of experience do you have? + −

Over 25 years. I have worked as a professional photographer since the 1990s, initially in the concert and music world (including for Mojo and North Sea Jazz) and now primarily in corporate photography. That background means fast reactions in dynamic situations — an advantage at events and reportage assignments.

Do you work with regular clients? + −

Yes. Many clients work with me on an ongoing basis for events, portraits or corporate reportage. That ensures recognisable imagery, efficient planning and less briefing per assignment. I am happy to think along about a consistent visual language over the longer term.

Do you work with international clients? + −

Yes, regularly. I work for international organisations, embassies and English-speaking clients. Communication can be in Dutch or English. This English version of the site provides more information for international clients.
